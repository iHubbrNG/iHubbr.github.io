More about iHubbr

iHubbr is an acceleration platform that trains entrepreneurs, and equip help them in getting fundings for their idea. The program is free and open to all entrepreneurs and aspiring entrepreneurs. It is a collaboration between Silicon Valley Companies, and venture capitalists across the globe.
All entrepreneurs and aspiring entrepreneurs are eligible. You just need a business idea (or an existing business that you wish to expand) and proficiency in English.
Learn to build a business and to apply for funding from us. Write a business proposal and have it evaluated by venture capitalists who have partnered with the program. The top proposals get to pitch to investors.

Pitch to investors at the final pitching event. The partnering venture capitalists decide which proposals to fund. Earn an all-expenses paid trip to any of our events in any part of the world to learn more and have the opportunity to take short business course at any of our partner universities.

Take a look at our launch event

November 2017 officially marked our partnership with GemsHub. ‘Leveraging on the power of technology for growth’ was a two-hour networking event and panel discussion for enterprenuers and aspiring enterprenuers, aimed at helping them create an online presence and growth using the latest growth strategy.


The Guest Speakers

Henry Neo, our Chief Operating Officer, gave the keynote speech and officially announced iHubbr's partnership with GemsHUb. Also presenting at the event were other industry professionals, including Jessy Hym and Evadon Forle, the co-founders of GemsHub Tech. The speakers shared their knowledge and experiences to a room full of attentive entrepreneurs and aspiring entreprenuers, who engaged with what they spoke about.
